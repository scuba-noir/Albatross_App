Welcome to the Albatross Community


We plan to partner with a collection of the best golf courses around the world to offer exclusive benefits. These benefits include the ability for owners to participate in special IRL events, enjoy exclusive discounts, benefit from early access to items, network with other community members and own unique digital pieces of art. We aim to foster a community of golf enthusiasts by giving them a platform to share their experiences, stories and passion with others.

Follow our social media accounts for roadmap and community updates!

Albatross Roadmap


Q1 2023

Team Building and Initial Tech Development

  • Finalize team size, members and roles ahead of 2023
  • Develop Albatross application version 1.0 to support utility functions
  • Begin development of Web3 technologies needed to support community
Q2 2023

Partnerships & Marketing

  • Secure partnership with targeted brands and media consultants
  • Begin to build community through targeted marketing efforts
  • Initiate discussions with potential golf courses
Q3 - Q4 2023

Further Development & Initial Mint

  • Secure first golf course partnership
  • Mint first Albatross NFT collection tied to real world utility
Beyond 2023

Expand Collections & Partnerships

  • Iterate on brand, tech and product in a goal to partner with 5 golf courses by the end of 2025 all with different NFT collections
  • First real world Albatross golf tournament for owner community
